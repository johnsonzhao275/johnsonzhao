About Me

My name is Johnson Zhao. I am currently a 4th Year student at Drexel University studying Computer Engineering. I am on track to graduate in the Spring of 2021. I went to Central High School and graduated back in the year 2016. (2 7 5 B A B Y).

I have been playing games since a very young age. When I was 8, my parents bought me my first computer. It was a Compaq Desktop that had Windows 98 on it. I still own my very first Playstation 1, Gameboy Color, Gameboy SD, Nintendo Wii, and more. Some of my favorite computer games include Maplestory, Crossfire, Rumble Fighter, Elsword, Runescape, and many more.

One of my most memorable upbringings is being apart of the Philadelphia SUNS, a non-profit organization based in Chinatown that helps to build character in it's members to become postitive influencers in the Philadelphia community. I am honored to be apart of this great organization ran by Harry Leong. Here, I was able to flourish my personal and technical skills through 10 years of competitive Basketball experience and 7 years of competitive Volleyball experience.

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is the organization that I am most active in at Drexel University. I was SASE's 2018-2019 Webmaster and is currently SASE's Treasurer for the 2019-2020 school year. I am also a peer mentor for 3 underclassmen in SASE's Mentorship Program. I am so thankful for my 3 mentees, Darian Shi, Johnny Ting, and Matthew Taing.
